FlavorMapping

This isn't just a culinary tool; it's a deep-dive into the "why" behind the ingredients we eat. I put a nerdy spin on the standard flavor mapping concept by integrating molecular structures and compositions. The goal was to visualize exactly what's inside our food, especially to explain the science behind why certain people can't eat specific items.

Key Features

  • Molecular Composition: View the chemical makeup of ingredients to understand their flavor and biological impact.
  • Allergen Science: Dedicated tracking that links sensitivities back to specific molecular structures.
  • Gastronomic Analysis: Assists in pairing ingredients by identifying shared molecular commonalities.

Use Cases

A bridge between culinary arts and food science, perfect for chefs, food enthusiasts, and individuals with dietary restrictions who want to understand the chemistry of their plate.
