DevCamp Research & Metrics

This platform serves as the foundational research hub for our Hackathon entry. It houses the critical metric data and statistical analysis that drove our development decisions. By analyzing market gaps and user needs, we were able to strategically justify the features and architecture of our final website.

Key Components

  • Metric Aggregation: Centralized dashboard for all data points collected during the pre-hackathon phase.
  • Strategic Rationale: Detailed explanations of the "why" behind our project, linking data to development priorities.
  • Market Gap Analysis: Visualization of existing solution limitations and how our proposal addresses them.

Hackathon Context

This project was the intellectual engine of our team. It ensured that our Hackathon website wasn't just built on assumptions, but was a data-driven solution tailored to real-world performance metrics.
