LGU-Kiosk (Commissioned Project)

This project was commissioned by a Local Bulacan LGU to address critical challenges in public service delivery. Designed to augment limited manpower, the kiosk provides an efficient, tech-fueled alternative to traditional over-the-counter processing, ensuring that all residents can be accommodated fairly and quickly.

Key Features

  • Government Assistance Portal: Streamlined workflows for Education, Financial, Medical, Burial, and Housing assistance.
  • Integrated Document Capture: Built-in support for scanning documents and taking ID photos directly at the kiosk.
  • Self-Service Documentation: Real-time generation and printing of official records like Barangay Certificates.
  • Multilingual Support: Full accessibility in English, Filipino, and Cebuano to serve diverse community members.

Intended Use Case

A vital digital bridge for community hubs, helping local governments move toward a more technology-driven environment while maintaining high-quality citizen support despite staffing constraints.
